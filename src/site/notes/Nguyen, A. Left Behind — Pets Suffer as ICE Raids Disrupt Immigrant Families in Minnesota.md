# Left Behind: Pets Suffer as ICE Raids Disrupt Immigrant Families in Minnesota
Anna Nguyen. February 6, 2025. [Source](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196)
- "[[Operation Metro Surge\|Operation Metro Surge]]'s collateral damage goes beyond affected families — to their beloved pets" ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))
- "'We started noticing an increase in stray and abandoned animals, dogs running loose with leashes and collars, in good condition, but no one claiming them, starting this September,' Kerry D'Amato, executive director of Pet Haven in St. Paul, told Sahan Journal" ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))
- "As ICE activity intensified, neighbors and friends of those targeted began bringing animals to Pet Haven, sometimes gathering several pets from multiple households" ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))
- "'It's a big team effort. It was always in the back of our mind as a rescue and as pet lovers,' she said. 'ICE doesn't have a social work department. There's nobody to come and get those animals. The people bringing them in are neighbors, family, friends, those who know the house is now empty, but remember there's an animal in there'" ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))
- "The agency is also seeing fewer pet vaccine and clinic appointments, and an increase in no-shows for veterinary appointments" ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))
- "'For someone who doesn't feel comfortable coming to us, they may need a third party to hep bring their animal to us, and we can call them on the phone to get ahold of them afterwards," Brayshaw said, "'It might not be our normal day-to-day, but we make it work in that situation" ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))
- "rescue organizations have stepped up to deliver pet food and basic supplies." ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))
	- "'We realized people weren't leaving their homes, weren't working, were losing income, and having to make difficult choices between feeding themselves and feeding their pets,' D'Amato said. 'We decided we needed to step up and be pet food-specific'" ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))
- "Thursday morning (Jan. 29), our phone line started blowing up'" D'Amato said, "'We have an encrypted form taht you can fill out for help. By yesterday (Feb. 4), we had delivered to 112 families and helped another 37 walking through our door, specifically affected by ICE" ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))
- "Pet Haven continues to collect pet food or supplies at its St. Paul location. Online donations are accepted through its website. Chewy and Amazon wish lists make it easy for donors to send food directly to the resuce" ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))

	- "It maintains a steady supply of emergency pet food and basic supplies at its Minneapolis pet food shelf location during open hours. No documentation is required to get pet food" ([Nguyen, 2025](https://sahanjournal.com/immigration/rescue-organizations-help-pets-affected-by-ice-surge-minnesota/?utm_source=Sahan+Journal+newsletter&utm_campaign=a57248a196-Sahan+Sunday_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-4785ce0c9c-633894526&mc_cid=a57248a196))
