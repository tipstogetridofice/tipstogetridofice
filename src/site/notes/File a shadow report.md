# How to File a Shadow Report to the UN
- "For example, when reporting violations of immigrants' rights in the United States to the Human Rights Committee, reference should be made to Article 13 of the [[International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights\|International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights]], which discusses the rights of aliens. When preparing a submission, there is no obligation to assess complaince under every article of a treaty; rather, it is best for advocates to focus on areas within their expertise where there is evidence of a particular violation" ([Source](https://www.theadvocatesforhumanrights.org/Res/dhr_training_manual_2017%203.pdf), p. 22)
