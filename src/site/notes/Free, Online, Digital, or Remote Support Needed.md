# Free Online, Digital, or Remote Actions
Actions you can take at your computer, from afar, and without spending money. Devote your time and attention to the resistance. 
### **Urgent: [[Help Minneapolis\|Help Minneapolis]]**
## Gum Up the Works 
Fascism thrives on efficiency and speed to overwhelm the resistance. **Activists operating against the Third Reich dedicated time and effort towards slowing down administrative processes. Be slow, be inefficient, be inconvenient.
